About Sea Glass

How is it made?

  1. Glass bottles and fisherman floats break and sink to the bottom of the ocean.
  2. The ocean water slowly erodes the edges of the glass creating a smooth stone like texture
  3. The glass is washed up onto shore for us to find

History

  1. "Mermaid Glass"
  2. Ancient Mesopotamia, Egypt, Syria, and Rome

Best places in the world for seaglass?

Glass Beach, Fort Bragg, California USA
Early inhabitants practiced throwing their trash over the cliffs in this area. The beach is covered in large ammounts of seaglass.
Seaham Beach, Seaham, England
This town was once home to Londonderry Bottle Works, which was made of 6 glass houses. Hundreds of years later after these glass factories closed glass still washes up on shore
Sea Glass/ Black Bay Beach, Ireland Island, Bermuda
The glass here is a result of a former glass factory, and can be find while snorkeling
